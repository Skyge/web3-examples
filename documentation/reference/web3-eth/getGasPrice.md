## getGasPrice
```js
web3.eth.getGasPrice([callback]);
```
> Returns the current gas price oracle.
> The gas price is determined by the last few blocks median gas price. GasPrice is the wei per unit of gas.
>
> <hr>
>
> #### Returns: `Promise<String>`
